Meet the Staff

Trish Rose is the Studio owner. She has danced for 20+ years. Her favorite type of dance has always been ballet and lyrical. She loves how graceful they both are. Trish teaches the company and hip hop classes.

You can often see Crystal at the reception desk. She is the daughter of Trish and runs the desk after school. She is also the instrustor for the tiny tots' classes. Her favorite style is Tap.

You will often find Alex at our studio. He is the jazz and tap instructor. He loves seeing the look on the kids faces when they manage to get a step right. Alex loves all the dance styles.

Rachel is also always at the studio! She completed the compant program and decided that she wanted to stay with the studio. Rachel teaches the ballet and lyrical classes. Rachel was one of the lost graceful students in the studio. Having her teach for us is such a treat!
