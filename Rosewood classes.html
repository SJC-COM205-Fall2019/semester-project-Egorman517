Classes

Tiny Tots

This is for ages 3-5 and specializes in parental seperation, hand eye coordination and major and fine motor skill development. This is all with a dance curriculumn.

Combo

These classes are an hour in length and hare a half hour of tap and half ballet. These classes are to prepare the students for a more serious dance education and show them the basic tap and ballet steps.

Tap

These classes consist of barre, center floor, rhythm and combination exercises. These are an hour in length. Have to be 7 years or older.

Jazz

These classes are offered to students 10 years or older. They are n hour in length and consist of warm- up floor , isolation and combination exercises. They are also offered in an hour and a half classes.

Ballet/ Lyrical

We offer ballet classes covering the royal systems. Barre, center floor, adagio and allegro are offered. classes can be an hour to an hour and a half in length. Lyrical is jazz and ballet mixed into one. must be taking ballet to take a lyrical class.
